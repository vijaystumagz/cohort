1 For whom this Entrepreneurship Cohort is?
The Entrepreneurship cohort is a specialized program initiated to support Students and budding entrepreneurs who are developing their startups.
2 When do I need to pay the amount?
You can make payment after the final list announcements at the end of the program. You only need to pay if you are selected.
3 Can I attend as a team?
Definitely! You can attend as a team of 3-4 members. The maximum limit is 4 members.
4 Whom do I need to contact if I face any problems/issues during payment or session?
You can contact us directly at Info@careerquotient.io in case of any issues or problems during the session or payment.
5 Is this an accelerator program?
This is NOT an accelerator program. Entrepreneurship cohort is an initiative to offer mentorship, support, and guidance to blooming startup owners in building a successful business.
6 How do I receive funding for my startup?
On completion of the 6-week program, the final list will be announced. Selected businesses will attend the funding rounds.
7 What if I don't get selected during the Entrepreneurship cohort's final announcement?
No worries! There will be another session where you can apply again and try your luck.
